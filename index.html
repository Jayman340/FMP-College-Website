Cybersecurity Threat Timeline: How Online Attacks Evolved

How attacks evolved — and how defence had to evolve.

About This Project

This website explores the evolution of cybersecurity threats over time, showing how attacks have developed from simple viruses to highly advanced cyber warfare and supply chain breaches. The timeline highlights key incidents such as ILOVEYOU, Stuxnet, WannaCry, and SolarWinds, demonstrating how attackers have adapted their techniques to exploit new technologies and systems.

Each event in the timeline explains how the attack worked, how it spread, and what impact it had. By analysing these incidents, the project shows how cybersecurity has had to evolve in response to increasingly complex threats.

Purpose

The purpose of this project is to help users understand how cyber threats have changed over time and why modern security measures are necessary. It aims to make technical concepts accessible while still providing accurate and detailed explanations of how attacks operate.

By examining both historical attacks and modern trends such as data extortion, the website highlights the shift from simple disruption to financially and politically motivated cybercrime.

What You Can Do

  • Explore the timeline to see how cyber threats have evolved
  • Use filters to compare different types of malware and attack methods
  • View detailed explanations of major cybersecurity incidents
  • Learn key terms in the glossary

Key Idea

Cybersecurity is constantly evolving. As technology improves, attackers develop new methods to bypass defences. Understanding past attacks helps organisations and individuals better prepare for future threats.

This project shows that cybersecurity is not just about protecting systems, but also about adapting to an ever-changing threat landscape.
